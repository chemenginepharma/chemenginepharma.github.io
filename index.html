    Chemengine Pharma Solutions Pvt. Ltd is an emerging leader in Organic chemistry and allied services. Chemengine Pharma Solutions is a research-based organisation involved in providing diverse product portfolio, chemistry solutions and custom synthesis services. 

    Chemengine Pharma solutions Pvt Ltd. offers a chemistry service of novel and unique building blocks for Drug Discovery, Active Pharmaceuticals Ingredients (APIs) Impurities, APIs process development, APIs key intermediates, Route scouting for API and advanced intermediates, research and development of new synthetic, semi-synthetic, total synthetic molecules, Agrochemical reference standard and its chemicals, fertilizers, petro-chemicals, cosmetics. Our high skilled, motivated team is having several years of experience in various Pharma Industries with hands on expertise in the field of Medicinal chemistry, Impurities synthesis, speciality chemicals, API’s, API intermediates and custom synthesis. We have capabilities to synthesize compound /material from lab scale (mg / g) to plant scale (Kg / Ton).
