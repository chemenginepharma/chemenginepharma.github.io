What We Do

Custom synthesis is the process where a molecule is made exclusively for a particular client according to specifications at their scale. It is one of the options available for pharmaceutical companies requiring active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), intermediates, fine chemicals, reference standards, impurities or metabolites needed to launch their research and development projects. Custom synthesis is essential to the pharmaceutical companies since many of these compounds can sometimes be very difficult to find or hard to synthesize. Custom synthesis provides the platform necessary for clients’ to accelerate their projects to market.

  • Chemengine Pharma offers a wide range of experience in the synthesis of mg to Kg scale molecules for chemical and pharmaceutical applications. Chemengine Pharma can meet your custom synthesis and active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), intermediates, fine chemicals, reference standards, impurities or metabolites.

Flow Chemistry

Custom Synthesis has been a specialty since the inception of Chemengine, and we are constantly advancing our capabilities and chemistry support services.

  • Where appropriate, we use Flow Chemistry Reactor Technology for custom synthesis projects. Potential benefits for our clients include increased scalability, flexibility, reaction speed, process control, and cost savings. Chemengine specializes in the custom synthesis of a wide range of small molecules including active pharmaceutical ingredients, pharmaceutical intermediates, drug intermediates, drug metabolites, drug analogs, analytical standards, reference standards and certified reference materials, chemical intermediates, chemical standards, and pharmaceutical impurities.
